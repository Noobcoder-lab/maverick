    About Us

    We at Mavericks belive there are no limits on who can become a great entrepreneur. You don't necessarily need a college degree, a bunch of money in the bank. However, you do need a strong plan and the drive to see it through. Our compassion, innovative thinking, determination and working on new ideas makes us different and unique.

    Our Mission

    Maverick Association aims for the people by providing them an oppurtunity to work and earn with the help of their mobile phones and in full comfort.

    Our Plan

    The internet is the phenomena that brought the world into our home. Our plan is to provide a system where people can make passive income by using social media.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to become the biggest association that could provide a source of passive income for life long. Our vision is to make the Indian youth aware about oppurtunities.
